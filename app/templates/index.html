Artificial Intelligence

In the present day, AI has a wide range of applications:
  • Commercial Uses (Computer Vision, VR)
  • Entertainment (Social Media Algorithms, AI-Generated Art)
As well as:
  • Law Enforcement (Face Recognition, Dispatch Systems)
  • Judicial Proceedings (Electronic Discovery, Recidivism Prediction)
This app will let you:
  • Create your own AI model to help judges predict cases of recidivism
  • Analyze how your model will impact other people
  • Show the effects of your model on individuals of different backgrounds
As you walk through this app think about:
  • How much trust can we put into AI without knowing how it was trained and on what dataset?
  • How does AI make choices?
  • Where in society should AI be used?
  • What happens when AI makes mistakes?

The best way to understand how Machine Learning is trained
and used is to try it yourself. You'll roleplay as a developer
in charge of creating a model for use in a court that helps
the judge make their decision with only the data the
local Justice Department provides you.
